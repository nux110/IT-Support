Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to the most common IT questions.

How do I reset my password? +
Go to the company login page and click "Forgot Password." You'll receive a reset link to your registered email. If you don't receive it, submit a ticket or call our help desk and we'll reset it manually after verifying your identity.
How do I connect to the VPN from home? +
Download the VPN client from the IT portal, install it, and log in with your corporate credentials. If you haven't been set up for VPN access yet, submit a request through this portal and we'll get you set up within 1 business day.
My computer is running slowly. What should I do? +
First, try restarting your computer — this resolves most performance issues. Make sure your OS and software are up to date. If the problem persists, submit a ticket with details about what's running slowly and when it started, and a technician will assess whether you need a hardware upgrade or software cleanup.
I accidentally deleted important files. Can they be recovered? +
Check the Recycle Bin first. If files were stored on network drives, we may be able to restore from backup — contact us immediately with the file names and folder paths. The sooner you report it, the better the chances of recovery. Do not save new files to the same location in the meantime.
I think I clicked a phishing link. What do I do? +
Don't panic — disconnect your computer from the network immediately (unplug the Ethernet or turn off Wi-Fi), then call our help desk right away. Do not shut down your computer as we may need to preserve evidence. We'll scan for malware, change your credentials, and determine if any data was compromised.
How do I set up my email on my phone? +
We support iOS and Android devices. Download the Microsoft Outlook app or use the native mail app with Exchange settings. Submit a ticket and a technician will walk you through the setup or enroll your device in our MDM system if required by company policy.
